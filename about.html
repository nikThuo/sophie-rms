Mission

Our mission is to enable youth in developing contexts achieve efficient and quality education using digital technologies.

Vision

To provide quality and equitable education through the use of digital technologies at the service of humanity and in inclusive ways.



Objectives

  1. Use technologies as a medium to educate the cognitive aspects of students, teachers, parents, education managers and communities.
  2. Employ technologies in developing the ethical dimension of students, teachers, parents, education managers and communities.
  3. Apply technologies in ways that educate the affective aspects of students, teachers, parents, education managers and communities.
  4. Utilize technologies to reach disadvantaged, physically and mentally challenged communities.
