Training


The training areas are specific to learning needs among teachers. While these may be generic, more specific areas will be addressed under the following themes:
  • Pedagogy (teaching methodology)
  • Assessment
  • Monitoring and Evaluation
  • Classroom management
  • Participatory approaches
  • Differentiation
  • Visual aids
  • Digital learning
  • Basics of the Competency Based Curriculum for teachers
  • Basics of the Competency Based Education for Parents
  • Feedback training forms(sub-categories): pre-training; mid-training; post-training

Pre-training (aspects-text boxes): which issues do you want discussed in the training(challenges); why are these issues important; how urgent is the issue in your school; who are the stakeholders responsible in addressing the issues

Mid-training: did the training cover the issues you expected?; which issues were missing; what were the aha moments; what can be done better; when was the training done(month, day, year)?

Post-training: was the training relevant; give reasons why the training was relevant or not relevant; which other issues do you think need to be covered; why do you think the other issues indicate should be covered; when was the training done(month, day, year)?

In this section resources will include:
  • Subject specific assessments (Past papers)
  • Marking schemes
  • Video lessons
  • Reading lists
  • Step-by-step guides
  • Subject specific lesson guides
  • Digital learning aids
