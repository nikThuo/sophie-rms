Pre-training (aspects-text boxes): which issues do you want discussed in the training(challenges); why are these issues important; how urgent is the issue in your school; who are the stakeholders responsible in addressing the issues

Mid-training: did the training cover the issues you expected?; which issues were missing; what were the aha moments; what can be done better; when was the training done(month, day, year)?

Post-training: was the training relevant; give reasons why the training was relevant or not relevant; which other issues do you think need to be covered; why do you think the other issues indicate should be covered; when was the training done(month, day, year)?
